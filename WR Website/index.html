Program Overview

A room to write, a catalyst for creativity, and a place where the gallant tigers meet the creatures of the sea…

The Writers’ Room at Minneapolis South High School is 826 MSP’s first satellite location. Here, South High students and faculty receive dedicated writing support from 826 MSP staff and volunteers through in-class tutoring, drop-in writing consultations, and hosting of workshops and events.
